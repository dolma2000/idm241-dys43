Interactive Blob

Reference from: UI Motion Kit
The Blob I will be improving from is seen at the starting screen from the UI Motion Kit website. Currently, the blob itself is not interactive and eventually proceeds to a new page.

How I want to Improve:

First I want it to be interactive. Once you hover over it, I want to make it so that the blob and text increase in size and the color of the blob will change too.

Below is a demo of how I want it to roughly look like. I wasn't able to find exactly what I wanted so I animated it in After Affect instead.

Trigger

Hovering above the Blob.

Rules

The Blob will increase in size and change to a vibrant gradient color. The text inside will also increase in size and change from black to white.

Feedback

Same as the rules, user will see the Blob and text get bigger, and have the color change too.

Loops & Modes

There is a loop - the blob will be constantly changing its shape slightly. There are no modes.
