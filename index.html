Micro-Interaction

Objective of Project:
To identiify micro interaction and design an improved and meaningful digital experience based on a holistic consideration of users experience. In this course, I will identify and improve three micro interactions in three different stages: Alpha(Simple), Beta(Slightly difficult), and Final(Complex).
Alpha (Simple): Hamburger Menu Bar
URL of original interface: My Portfolio Website
Currently, the Hamburger Menu Bar is just a static button that when pressed upon, the menu will slide in form the left covering over half of the page's width. How I want to Improve: First, I want the Hamburger Icon to transitiion to an "X" Icon through animation. Second, I want the other links in the menu bar to slide in one by one like a ripple as shown below.

Trigger: Tapping on the Hamburger Icon.
Rules: After tapping on the Hamburger Icon, it reveals the Menu Bar.
Feedback: The Hamburger Icon will transition into an X and the whole menu bar will appear sliding from the left, with the links coming in one by one.
Loops & Modes: The mode here is the Menu Bar appearing with extra information, which are the 3 links.
